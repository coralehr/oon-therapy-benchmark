Out-of-network estimator

What might my insurance reimburse for therapy?

If you see a therapist who isn’t in your insurance network, your plan may pay you back for part of the cost. This tool gives you a transparent, ballpark range — built entirely from public CMS data, with every number shown. It is an estimate, not a guarantee.

The calculator needs its data file (therapy_oon_benchmark_v0.json), and it didn’t load. This usually happens when the page is opened directly from disk (browsers block local file reads) rather than served over HTTP.

To fix it, serve the folder over a local web server:

  1. Open a terminal in the project root.
  2. Run python3 -m http.server 8000
  3. Visit http://localhost:8000/examples/calculator/

Prefer to check by hand? Look up the Medicare non-facility allowed amount for your CPT code and locality in data/therapy_oon_benchmark_v0_by_locality.csv, then multiply by your plan’s coinsurance percentage. The out-of-network band in v0 is Medicare × 1–2 (a documented placeholder).
